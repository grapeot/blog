## About

I'm Yan Wang (鸭哥). I've spent ~20 years in AI research and engineering — Columbia PhD, 40+ academic papers, 20+ US patents. And I now focus on what happens when AI stops being a tool you use and starts being a collaborator you manage.

This blog, *Computing Life*, has been running since 2012. Over 370 articles, bilingual ([English](https://yage.ai/tag/english.html) / [中文](https://yage.ai/tag/chinese.html)), covering AI methodology and engineering practice. Occasionally astrophotography. The recurring theme: how to think clearly about technology and build systems that actually work.

A few ideas I keep returning to:

- **Context Engineering**: the quality of AI output is determined by the context you construct, not the prompt you write
- **AI Management**: treating AI agents the way you'd manage a team — with delegation, review, and escalation
- **Builder's Mindset**: the gap between using AI tools and building with AI is where the real leverage lives

I co-founded [Superlinear Academy](https://superlinear.academy), where we teach AI Builders through courses and an active community (3000+ alumni). The courses are practical and project-based: AI Coding, AI Builders, AI Architect, AI Product.

If you want to get in touch, the best way is through the Superlinear Academy community or a comment on any blog post. You can also find me on [GitHub](https://github.com/grapeot).

## 关于

我是鸭哥。做了大概20年AI研发，哥伦比亚大学PhD，发过40+篇论文，拿过20+个美国专利。现在关注的问题是：当 AI 从你用的工具变成你管理的协作者，工作方式到底怎么变。
